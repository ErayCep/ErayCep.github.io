Rusty-Chip8 Details

This is a chip8 emulator developed with Rust Programming Language. Chip-8 was the first processor used in video games. Due to it's rather simple nature, and my interest in low level programming I tried to develop an emulator for it. I used SDL2 for windowing and chip-8 reference manual for programming.
